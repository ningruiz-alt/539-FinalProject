How games shaped Me

This site is an archive of the games that have stayed with me. From childhood to now, they have quietly shaped how I interact with the world.

Games that I like

Open World · Story

Red Dead Redemption 2

Made my first trip to the Rockies feel strangely familiar. I recalled many scenes from this game when I was travelling around Banff. Impressive narration and game art.

Narrative · Choices

Detroit: Become Human

A giant decision tree hidden behind very small choices. I played this game 3 times by trying different style endings, one of the reasons that I came to ann arbor to study.

Cooporation games

RainbowSix Seige

Company with me through out my undergrad journey and reconnected me with my high school friends. Spent over 1700 hours on this game. Couldn't say I regreted but I did enjoy the time I spent with my friends. Not recommonded playing these days since I dont think Ubisoft is running it well.
